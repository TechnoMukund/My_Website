Tirunelveli, a city steeped in history and culture, offers a unique blend of experiences for para tourists. From exploring ancient temples to trekking through wildlife sanctuaries, there's something for everyone.

Agastiyar Falls

Cascading down a series of seven tiers from a height of 300 feet, Manimuthar Falls is a visual masterpiece. Surrounded by lush greenery, the plunge pool at the base of the falls is ideal for a refreshing dip.

Nellaiyapar Temple

Steeped in history and architectural grandeur, the Arulmigu Nellaiappar Temple is the heart and soul of Tirunelveli. Dedicated to Lord Shiva, worshipped here as Nellaiappar, the temple complex is a labyrinthine marvel. Twin towers, or gopurams, pierce the sky, adorned with intricate carvings depicting scenes from mythology.
